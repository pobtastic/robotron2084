E2FD: Clamp Entity Velocity
Used by the routines at Process_Tracked_Enemy_Entity and Spawn_Tracked_Enemy_Entities.
Adds a 16-bit signed delta (DE) to a velocity byte in H, sign-extending H to a full 16-bit value first, then clamps the result to the range FC00-03FF. Returns the clamped result in HL. Called from Process_Tracked_Enemy_Entity and Process_Player_Tracking_Enemy when updating tracked enemy velocities.
Clamp_Entity_Velocity E2FD LD A,H Sign-extend H into a 16-bit value and add the delta in DE.
