C544: Print Player Score
Used by the routines at Clear_Colour_Bar_Column and RenderHUD.
Renders the active player score from packed BCD bytes at Player_1_State, terminated by FF. Uses PrintDispatch with 16 (PRINT AT): row cursor in C starts from *C414 and advances by 06 per digit row; column is 02. High and low nibbles may each yield a digit; calls UpdateAttributeBar_FillBorder after each completed score line. Used from Erase_Narrow_Entity_Sprite and RenderHUD.
