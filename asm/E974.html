E974: Kill Wall Bouncing Enemy
Used by the routine at Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy.
Reached when a wall-bouncing enemy makes contact with the player. Saves the entity position in Entity_Record_Pointer, erases the sprite via Erase_Narrow_Entity_Sprite, and removes the entity from the list via Remove_Entity_From_List. Decrements the wave enemy total at Active_Enemy_Count and the bouncing enemy count at Bouncing_Enemy_Count. Triggers the border colour effect via Initialise_Entity_State and awards 05 BCD points. Refreshes the colour bar, double-erases the sprite via Erase_Wide_Entity_Sprite and Draw_Wide_Entity_Sprite_Pixels_Only, writes the dying handler Animate_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy_Death into the record, and returns.
