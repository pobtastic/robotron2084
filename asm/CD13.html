CD13: Advance Entity Pointer
Used by the routine at Remove_Entity_From_List.
Written into entity records as the per-frame handler once an entity has been processed. Advances HL by 0A bytes to step past the current record and returns to the entity dispatcher.
Advance_Entity_Pointer CD13 LD DE,$000A Add 0A to HL to step past the current entity record.
CD16 ADD HL,DE
CD17 RET Return to the entity dispatcher.
