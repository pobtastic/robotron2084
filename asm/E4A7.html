E4A7: Retire Expired Entity
Used by the routine at Tick_Entity_Colour_Timer.
Erases the entity sprite via Erase_Entity_Sprite, writes the Process_Tracked_Enemy_Entity_Advance advance handler to the entity record (retiring the entity from active update processing), and returns through Retire_Expired_Entity_Advance. Reached when a colour timer spawned by Kill_Tracked_Enemy_Entity expires.
