ED10: Save Or Load High Scores
Used by the routines at Display_High_Score_Entry_Screen and Attract_Mode_Loop.
Checks whether J (save) or L (load) is held on keyboard row BF. On J, saves the 012D-byte score table from Messaging_HighScoreName to tape. On L, loads from tape into the buffer at Entity_List_Base; a successful load copies the buffer to the live score data and restarts the display. A failed load shows a tape error message — or a wrong-game warning if a Moon Patrol score table was detected — then waits for a keypress before returning.
