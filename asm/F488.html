F488: Draw Colour Flash Rectangle
Used by the routine at Extra_Life_Flash.
Renders one frame of the extra life colour flash animation. The frame index in A controls the inset from the edges: 0B paints a narrow band near the centre of the attribute area; 00 fills the full screen border. Each call fills the top row, the left and right border cells of each middle row, and the bottom row with the flash colour.
Input
A Frame index (00-0B); lower values produce a wider rectangle
