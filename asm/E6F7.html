E6F7: Process Player Tracking Enemy
Used by the routine at Process_Cluster_Entity.
Per-frame entity handler for the slow player-tracking enemy. Tests player contact via HitTestBounds8AtHL; triggers death via Strip_Attributes_And_Fade if touching. Computes the signed Y and X distance from the player at C4D4 and C4D5 halves it five times to derive a velocity nudge, and negates if the enemy is above or to the left of the player. Reads the entity facing byte; on a random 1-in-8192 chance sets it to 1A (freeze state). If the facing is 1A flips both velocity components and tests for a 1-in-256 chance to clear the freeze. Applies the computed Y and X nudge to the entity position, clamping to the play area, and draws the entity via Draw_Entity_Sprite.
