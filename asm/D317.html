D317: Render HUD
Used by the routines at Stage_Next_Wave and PlayerDeath.
Clears the screen buffer, sets a white background, draws the playfield border (DrawPlayfieldBorder), and redraws the attribute bar (UpdateAttributeBar) before falling through to RenderHUD_Draw. Scores (PrintPlayerScore) and lives (DrawLivesDisplay) are then rendered for each player in turn by swapping state with Swap_Player_State; the "WAVE" label and wave number (PrintWaveNumber) are printed last for player 1.
RenderHUD D317 CALL ClearScreenBuffer Call ClearScreenBuffer.
D31A LD A,$07 Call SetBackgroundColour using WHITE.
D31C CALL SetBackgroundColour
D31F CALL DrawPlayfieldBorder Call DrawPlayfieldBorder.
D322 CALL UpdateAttributeBar Call UpdateAttributeBar.
