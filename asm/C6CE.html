C6CE: Lookup Vector Table Entry
Returns the word at index A in Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table: HL = Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table + (A * 02), little-endian word into DE. Valid range is Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table through C6CD (last word starts at C6CC). No direct CALL to LookupVectorTableEntry in this ROM.
LookupVectorTableEntry C6CE ADD A,A Double A (word stride).
C6CF LD HL,$C60C HL=Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table (table base).
C6D2 LD D,$00 D=00.
C6D4 LD E,A E=A (word offset in DE).
C6D5 ADD HL,DE Add DE to HL (pointer to the entry).
C6D6 LD E,(HL) E=*HL (low byte of the word).
C6D7 INC HL Advance HL.
C6D8 LD D,(HL) D=*HL (high byte of the word).
C6D9 RET Return with the word in DE.
