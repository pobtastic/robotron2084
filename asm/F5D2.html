F5D2: Flash Colour Cycle Table
Seven-step colour rotation table used by Advance_Flash_Colour_Cycle. Each byte maps to the next in the cycle: blue (09) to green (24) to cyan (2D) to white (3F) to yellow (36) to magenta (1B) to red (12) and back to blue. The eighth byte repeats the first to wrap the cycle.
