D794: Draw Clipped Wide Sprite
Used by the routines at Clear_Colour_Bar_Column and Animate_Intro_Screen_Sprites.
Draws a 2-byte-wide, 12-row entity sprite to the screen by toggling each row's pixel data against the existing screen content. Reads the sprite type and screen position from the entity record, looks up the pixel data address in Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table, then renders twelve rows of two bytes each. Each row is bounds-checked against the play area (10 top / B9 bottom); rows outside the play area are skipped. Alternate registers carry the Y position and column offset across all twelve rows.
