C5C1: Check Score Award Life
Used by the routine at Main_Game_Loop.
Checks whether the player's score has reached the extra-life threshold. Compares the four BCD score bytes at Player_1_State against the threshold at ExtraLifeCompare_b0. Returns immediately if the score falls below the threshold on any byte. When the threshold is reached, awards one extra life, refreshes the life icons, and raises the threshold by BCD 02 ready for the next award. Called from Erase_Narrow_Entity_Sprite.
