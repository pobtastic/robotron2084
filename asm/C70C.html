C70C: Help Bar Attribute Sequence
Seven attribute bytes (41 / 42 / 44 ink cycling) and a 00 terminator, consumed from UpdateAttributeBar when UpdateAttributeBar_ResetTable arms HL here after the "H" key path through PollHKey.
HelpBarAttributeSequence C70C DEFB $41 INK: BLUE, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C70D DEFB $41 INK: BLUE, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C70E DEFB $42 INK: RED, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C70F DEFB $42 INK: RED, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C710 DEFB $44 INK: GREEN, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C711 DEFB $44 INK: GREEN, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
C712 DEFB $00 Terminator.
