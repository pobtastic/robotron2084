F0EE: Cycle Border Colour
Used by the routines at Display_High_Score_Entry_Screen and Save_Or_Load_High_Scores.
Advances the border colour cycle one step and redraws the attribute border frame. Reads the current step from Border_Colour_Step, decrements it (wrapping at 0 back to 7), looks up the outer/inner attribute pair from Table_Border_Colour_Pairs, and calls Fill_Border_Attributes to repaint the border.
Cycle_Border_Colour F0EE LD A,($C508) Read and decrement the border colour step from Border_Colour_Step.
F0F1 DEC A
F0F2 AND %00000111 Mask the step to the range 0–7.
F0F4 LD ($C508),A Store the updated step back to Border_Colour_Step.
F0F7 SLA A Double the step to a word offset and index into the colour pair table at Table_Border_Colour_Pairs.
F0F9 LD C,A
F0FA LD B,$00
F0FC LD HL,$F0DE
F0FF ADD HL,BC
F100 LD D,(HL) Load the outer (D) and inner (E) attribute bytes from the table.
F101 INC HL
F102 LD E,(HL)
F103 CALL Fill_Border_Attributes Redraw the screen border with the new colour pair via Fill_Border_Attributes.
F106 RET Return.
