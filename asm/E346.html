E346: Apply Direction Delta
Used by the routine at Spawn_Tracked_Enemy_Entities.
Reads the direction byte from (DE), and uses it as an index into the direction animation table at Direction_Animation_Offset_Table (direction × 4). Adds the Y delta at table[index] to the byte at (DE+1) and the X delta at table[index+1] to the byte at (DE+2), writing the updated coordinates back in place. Called from Spawn_Tracked_Enemy_Entities_Record when building entity records.
Apply_Direction_Delta E346 EX DE,HL Exchange DE and HL and load the direction table base at Direction_Animation_Offset_Table.
