EE1D: Highlight Score Entry Row
Used by the routines at Initialise_Display_And_Border and Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor.
Writes INK: WHITE, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) (bright white on black) to 1C consecutive attribute cells from HL. Called to highlight a complete score entry row when the cursor moves.
Input
HL Pointer to the attribute area (advanced by 2 before writing)
Highlight_Score_Entry_Row EE1D INC HL Skip past the table position bytes and set the fill count to 1C.
EE1E INC HL
EE1F LD A,$1C
Score_Row_Highlight_Loop EE21 LD (HL),$47 Write INK: WHITE, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) to each attribute cell and loop until 1C cells are highlighted.
EE23 INC HL
EE24 DEC A
EE25 JR NZ,Score_Row_Highlight_Loop
EE27 RET Return.
