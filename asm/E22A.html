E22A: Begin Enemy Death Animation
Used by the routine at Handle_Horizontal_Enemy_Entity.
Called when a colliding wave enemy is struck: freezes movement, removes the sprite, flashes it white, marks it as dying, and starts the 32-frame death timer. Decrements the live count for this enemy's class. Called from Handle_Horizontal_Enemy_Entity and Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy.
