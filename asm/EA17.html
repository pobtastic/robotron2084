EA17: Handle Enemy Collision Chain
Used by the routine at Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy.
Reached from Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy when the bouncer finds no collision partner. Flags the entity as active, reads the entity type and state, decrements the slot count at Table_SlotCount, and installs the retire handler Process_Entity_Record_Next7. Then appends a replacement entity record (handler Process_Replacement_Enemy_Entity) to the entity list at *Entity_List_Write_Pointer, writes the list terminator Entity_List_Reset, and jumps to Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy_Advance to redraw without moving.
