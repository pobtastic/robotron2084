E863: Evaluate and Spawn Tracked Enemies
Used by the routine at Stage_Next_Wave.
Entry point called from Stage_Next_Wave. Reads the tracked enemy active count at C42C; if non-zero, jumps immediately to the spawn step at Evaluate_And_Spawn_Tracked_Enemies_Go. Otherwise checks C42D; if zero, also jumps to Evaluate_And_Spawn_Tracked_Enemies_Go. If C42D is non-zero, halves it twice: if the result is still non-zero, jumps to Evaluate_And_Spawn_Tracked_Enemies_Go; otherwise increments A to 01 and falls through to store the spawn count and call Spawn_Tracked_Enemy_Entities.
