F5B7: Intro Colour Cycle Table
Sequence of ZX Spectrum attribute byte values cycling the intro screen display colours. Consecutive bytes form source/replacement pairs; FF is the end sentinel that wraps the pointer to the table start.
Intro_Colour_Cycle_Table F5B7 DEFB $42 INK: RED, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
F5B8 DEFB $43 INK: MAGENTA, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
F5B9 DEFB $46 INK: YELLOW, PAPER: BLACK (BRIGHT) .
F5BA DEFB $FF Terminator.
