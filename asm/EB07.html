EB07: Process Final Phase Enemy Entity
Used by the routine at Process_Replacement_Enemy_Entity.
Per-frame handler for the final phase of a wall-bouncing enemy, reached from Process_Replacement_Enemy_Entity when the replacement entity countdown expires. Spawns the entity with colour WHITE, calls Find_Colliding_Entity to search for a player-contact slot, and jumps to Process_Final_Phase_Enemy_Entity_Kill if none is found. Calls HitTestBounds8AtHL to test player contact and jumps to Strip_Attributes_And_Fade to kill the player if touching. Steps back to the direction field and generates a random value; keeps the current direction if below F0 otherwise picks a new direction, wraps it within 33 and writes it back. Looks up Y and X deltas from Enemy_Movement_Delta_Table (direction & 03 × 2), validates the new position within the play area, and spawns the entity. If no contact slot is found, erases the sprite, removes the entity from the list, awards BCD score, and writes the retire handler.
