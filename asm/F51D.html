F51D: Advance Colour Cycle
Used by the routine at Attract_Mode_Loop.
Advances the colour cycling animation by one step. Scans all 768 screen attribute cells and replaces cells matching 47 with the next colour from the sequence at Colour_Cycle_Table, then immediately replaces all cells matching the following entry with 47 to prime the next cycle step.
