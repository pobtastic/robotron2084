E003: Process Entity Record
Processes one entity slot in the wave entity list. If the slot is active, the enemy's animation colours are updated on screen via Clear_Colour_Bar_Column_26 and its animation counter decremented. If the counter expires the enemy is erased and the slot retired. If the slot holds spawn data (Process_Entity_Record_Inactive), a new enemy position is found and the enemy is placed in the play area.
Input
HL Pointer to the entity type/state byte
