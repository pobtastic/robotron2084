EAF7: Enemy Direction Code Table
Sixteen direction-code bytes used by Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy to map a packed 4-bit direction index (Y-sign × 4 | X-sign) to a colour attribute byte written into the wall-bouncing enemy record. Indexed as table + BC where BC = (Y_direction & 03) | (X_direction << 2).
