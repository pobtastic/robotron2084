EE0B: Calculate Attribute Cell Address
Used by the routines at Initialise_Display_And_Border and Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor.
Looks up a character index in the word table at 4600 to find its pixel row address, then converts that address to the corresponding attribute cell in the range 5800-5BFF by shifting the screen row right by 3 and combining with 58. Called by Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor to position score entry highlight attributes.
Input
A Character index
