F606: OR Blit Title Strips
Used by the routine at DrawAttractTitle.
OR-blits horizontal strips of bitmap data from the source pointer in HL into the screen buffer, using the four-byte parameter block at TitleBlit_Params–C4FC. *TitleBlit_Params seeds the outer strip counter in B; *C4FA drives the screen row lookup (with 8C00 / 8D style indexing); *C4FB is the byte count per strip (LDIR length); the loop runs until B reaches *C4FC after each pass. Called from DrawAttractTitle with HL pointing at ROM graphics (6D7D and 7005).
Input
HL Pointer to source bitmap data in ROM
