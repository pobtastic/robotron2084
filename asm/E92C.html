E92C: Spawn Wall Bouncing Enemies
Used by the routine at Stage_Next_Wave.
Called from Stage_Next_Wave. Reads the wall-bouncing enemy spawn count from *HL and stores it at Bouncing_Enemy_Count. Returns immediately if the count is zero. Otherwise adds the count to the wave enemy total at Active_Enemy_Count and spawns B bouncers, writing each entity record (handler Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy, entity type 04 random screen position via Pick_Random_Spawn_Position_Y, and state bytes 0F and 0A) into the entity list at *Entity_List_Write_Pointer. Closes the list with Entity_List_Reset and restores the wave data pointer.
