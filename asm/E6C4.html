E6C4: Apply Entity Velocity
Used by the routine at Process_Cluster_Entity.
Applies a pre-computed velocity nudge (BC) to the Y and X position bytes of the entity record, clamping each to the play area bounds, and redraws the entity via Draw_Narrow_Entity_Sprite. Called as the contact handler from Process_Cluster_Entity when a cluster entity reaches the player.
