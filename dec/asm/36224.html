36224: Plot Screen Pixel
Used by the routines at DrawPlayfieldBorderRow, DrawPlayfieldBorder, Fill_Row and Fill_Column.
Renders one pixel at screen position (H = pixel column, L = pixel row). Derives the column byte offset and pixel mask by indexing into Column_Pixel_Lookup_Table, and reads the screen scan-line address from Screen_Row_Address_Lookup_Table. Three entry points: PlotCharacterCell_Draw draws by setting bits, PlotCharacterCell_Erase erases by clearing bits, PlotCharacterCell_Toggle toggles bits.

H Pixel column
L Pixel row
