54529: Intro Sprite Template
Three four-byte entries used as the initial sprite state for Intro_Sprite_State. Copied to the working state at the start of each animation cycle by Animate_Intro_Screen_Sprites. Each entry contains the sprite identifier at +0 the initial Y-position at +1 the initial X-position at +2 and the X-velocity at +3.
