62982: OR Blit Title Strips
Used by the routine at DrawAttractTitle.
OR-blits horizontal strips of bitmap data from the source pointer in HL into the screen buffer, using the four-byte parameter block at TitleBlit_Params–50428. *TitleBlit_Params seeds the outer strip counter in B; *50426 drives the screen row lookup (with 35840 / 141 style indexing); *50427 is the byte count per strip (LDIR length); the loop runs until B reaches *50428 after each pass. Called from DrawAttractTitle with HL pointing at ROM graphics (28029 and 28677).

HL Pointer to source bitmap data in ROM
