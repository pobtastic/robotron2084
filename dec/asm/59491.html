59491: Evaluate and Spawn Tracked Enemies

Entry point called from Stage_Next_Wave. Reads the tracked enemy active count at 50220; if non-zero, jumps immediately to the spawn step at Evaluate_And_Spawn_Tracked_Enemies_Go. Otherwise checks 50221; if zero, also jumps to Evaluate_And_Spawn_Tracked_Enemies_Go.
