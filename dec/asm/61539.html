61539: Draw Colour Ring
Used by the routine at Draw_Concentric_Rings.
Draws one rectangular colour ring with its top-left corner at column B, row C. The horizontal edges span columns B to 255-B and are drawn via Fill_Row; the vertical edges span rows C to 191-C and are drawn via Fill_Column.
Input
BC Corner offset (B = column, C = row)
Draw the top and bottom horizontal edges.

61540 LD L,C

61542 SLA A Calculate the horizontal edge length as 256 minus 2 times the column offset.
