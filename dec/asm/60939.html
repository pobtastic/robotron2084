60939: Calculate Attribute Cell Address
Used by the routines at Initialise_Display_And_Border and Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor.
Looks up a character index in the word table at 17920 to find its pixel row address, then converts that address to the corresponding attribute cell in the range 22528-23551 by shifting the screen row right by 3 and combining with 88. Called by Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor to position score entry highlight attributes.
