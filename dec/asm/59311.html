59311: Direction Animation Offset Table
Eight 4-byte entries (one per direction 0-7) used by Build_Entity_Spawn_Record and Apply_Direction_Delta. Each entry holds a Y delta, an X delta, and two further bytes that are copied verbatim into the entity spawn buffer. Indexed as table + (direction × 4).
