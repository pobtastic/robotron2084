55983: Draw Wide Entity Sprite
Used by the routines at Clear_Colour_Bar_Column, Kill_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy and Animate_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy_Death.
Draws a 3-byte-wide, 16-row entity sprite to the screen by toggling each row's pixel data against the existing screen content. Applying this routine twice draws and then erases a sprite, leaving the screen unchanged. Row screen addresses come from the row address lookup table at Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table, offset by the entity's column position. Rows outside the play area (16 top / 176 bottom) are skipped. Called to draw and erase entity sprites during normal gameplay and death animations.
