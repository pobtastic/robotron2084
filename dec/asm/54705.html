54705: Remove Entity from List
Used by the routines at Process_Enemy_Entity, Clear_Colour_Bar_Column, Kill_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy and Process_Final_Phase_Enemy_Entity.
Removes the entity whose record is pointed to by Entity_Record_Pointer from the entity list. Steps back to the start of the entity's 12-byte record, then block-copies all subsequent records down by 12 bytes via LDIR, closing the gap. The entity list end pointer at Playfield_Entity_List_Pointer is decremented by 12 to reflect the shorter list. Returns with HL pointing to the record that now occupies the removed entity's slot.
