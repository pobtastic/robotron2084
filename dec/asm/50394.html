50394: Entity Record Pointer
Pointer to the entity record currently being processed. Set before calling Remove_Entity_From_List to identify which entity to remove, and saved on entry to the movement handler at Move_And_Redraw_Entity so that the entity can be located after sprite-draw calls that may disturb HL.
