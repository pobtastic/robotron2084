50963: Update Attribute Bar
Used by the routines at Main_Game_Loop, Clear_Colour_Bar_Column, RenderHUD and PlayerDeath.
Paints the HUD attribute border with *Border_Colour and writes the cycling colour from HelpBarAttributeSequence to 7 attribute cells at *Colour_Bar_Column. Checks the H key (help) and F key (restart). UpdateAttributeBar_FillBorder is also called directly by 50621.
