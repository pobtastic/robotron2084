60360: Display High Score Entry Screen
Used by the routines at Check_Any_Key_During_Display and Attract_Mode_Loop.
Initialises the high score entry screen: clears the display, prints the option header from Messaging_SelectOptions, and renders the current score entries with their cursor markers. Runs a 75-frame input loop allowing keyboard and joystick adjustment of the three score entry fields via Adjust_Score_Field_Cursor and Adjust_Name_Entry_Cursor. After name entry, redraws the final score table and cycles the display for 50 frames before jumping to the attract mode at Attract_Mode_Loop.
