63293: Read Control Fuller
Control mode 4: reads the Fuller Box joystick port 127 (active- high bits; fire is at bit 7) and remaps direction and fire bits to the game's active-low format.
Read_Control_Fuller 63293 IN A,(127) Read from the joystick;
Port Number Bit
0 1 2 3 4
127 UP DOWN LEFT RIGHT -
63295 OR %01110000 Mask to the relevant direction bits.
