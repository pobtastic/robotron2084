57634: Spawn Wave Enemies
Used by the routine at Stage_Next_Wave.
Reads three spawn-count bytes from HL (slots 16 32 48) and populates the entity list at *Entity_List_Working_Pointer with entities via Spawn_Wave_Enemies_Populate_Slot. Each record has a random screen position and a slot-typed variant; Draw_Wide_Entity_Sprite spawns each entity. Terminates the list with 53668 and updates *Entity_List_Write_Pointer.
