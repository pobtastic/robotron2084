50946: Dispatch Handler Pointer From HL
Used by the routines at Main_Game_Loop, Clear_Colour_Bar_Column and Stage_Next_Wave.
Chained trampoline: pushes resume address DispatchWordFromHL, loads a 22-bit routine pointer from HL, advances HL by 2 then jumps to that pointer via the stack. When the handler RETs, execution continues at DispatchWordFromHL for the next table entry, so handlers should leave HL valid for the following word unless the chain ends. Used from 52319, 52927, 53061, 53884 with tables such as Entity_List_Base.
