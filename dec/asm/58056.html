58056: Pick Random Spawn Position
Used by the routines at Spawn_Horizontal_Wave_Enemies, Spawn_Tracked_Enemy_Entities and Spawn_Wall_Bouncing_Enemies.
Generates a random Y coordinate in the range 16-167 and a random X coordinate in the range 8-231 writing both to *HL with initial velocities 16/16 then tests whether the chosen position overlaps the player spawn zone via HitTestBounds8AtHL; retries the whole selection if it does. Called from Spawn_Horizontal_Wave_Enemies and Spawn_Wall_Bouncing_Enemies.
