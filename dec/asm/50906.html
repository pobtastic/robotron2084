50906: Hit Test: 8-Byte Bounds Pair
Used by the routines at Primary_Enemy_Entity_Handler, Find_Colliding_Entity, Clear_Colour_Bar_Column, Spawn_Primary_Wave_Enemies, Generate_Random_Byte, Process_Entity_Record_Fast, Handle_Horizontal_Enemy_Entity, Find_Colliding_Wave_Enemy, Pick_Random_Spawn_Position_Y, Process_Tracked_Enemy_Entity, Process_Cluster_Entity, Process_Player_Tracking_Enemy, Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy and Process_Final_Phase_Enemy_Entity.
Reads four little-endian words at HL (8 bytes). The first two load into main BC and DE; EXX swaps to the shadow set so the next two words load into shadow BC and DE while HL walks the same block. After swapping back, four ADD/SUB pairs (with further EXX) each end in RET C if the subtraction borrows (no hit). If none borrow, falls through to RET with carry clear (hit). HL ends just past the eighth byte. Used throughout the gameplay object loop for collisions (among others 52442, 52517, 53577, 53805, 57405, 60188).
