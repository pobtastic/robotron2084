53948: Strip Attributes and Fade
Used by the routines at Primary_Enemy_Entity_Handler, Clear_Colour_Bar_Column, Handle_Horizontal_Enemy_Entity, Process_Tracked_Enemy_Entity, Process_Cluster_Entity, Process_Player_Tracking_Enemy, Process_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy and Process_Final_Phase_Enemy_Entity.
Player-death transition. Strips the paper and bright bits from every attribute cell in the attribute area (22528–23295), keeping only the ink colour, then calls Fade_Screen to fade the screen pixel data to black with a flashing border effect. On return, two levels of call stack are discarded via back-to-back POP DE, aborting the current game-loop callers and returning to the outer death-handling code.
