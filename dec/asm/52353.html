52353: Draw Spawned Entity
Used by the routines at Process_Enemy_Entity and Spawn_Primary_Wave_Enemies.
Reads the sprite type and screen address from the entity record at HL, looks up the sprite pixel data in Sprite_Pixel_Data_Table, then XOR-blits eight pixel rows onto the screen. Falls through to Draw_Spawned_Entity_Attribute to write the entity's attribute colour from the colour cycle table. Called immediately after a new entity is written to the entity list.
