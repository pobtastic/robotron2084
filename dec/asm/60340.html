60340: Enemy Movement Delta Table
Four 2-byte entries (Y delta, X delta) used by Process_Final_Phase_Enemy_Entity to move the final-phase enemy one step in one of four diagonal directions. Indexed as table + (direction & 3) × 2. The four entries encode movement by 3 pixels: up-left (253 0), right (0 3), down (3 0), and left (0 253).
Enemy_Movement_Delta_Table 60340 DEFB 253,0
60342 DEFB 0,3
60344 DEFB 3,0
60346 DEFB 0,253
