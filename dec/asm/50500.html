50500: Print Player Score
Used by the routines at Clear_Colour_Bar_Column and RenderHUD.
Draws the player's score on screen from the packed BCD bytes at Player_1_State (two decimal digits per byte), terminated by 255. Each digit occupies six screen rows; the display row starts at *50196 and advances by 6 per digit. All digits are printed at column 2 via PrintDispatch. After the last digit the score display attributes are refreshed by UpdateAttributeBar_FillBorder. Called from Erase_Narrow_Entity_Sprite and RenderHUD.
