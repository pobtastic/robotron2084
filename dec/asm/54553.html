54553: Animate Intro Screen Sprites
Used by the routine at Main_Game_Loop.
Controls a three-sprite scrolling animation on the intro screen. Entering at Animate_Intro_Screen_Sprites copies the sprite template to the working state and sets the frame counter to 14. Entering at Animate_Intro_Screen_Sprites_Step runs one frame: each sprite is rendered via Draw_Clipped_Wide_Sprite, its Y-position scrolled down by 6 and X-position advanced by its velocity. After 14 frames, a bright white closing sprite is drawn and the animation restarts.
