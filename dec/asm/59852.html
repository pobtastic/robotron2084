59852: Animate Wall Bouncing Enemy Death
Per-frame death handler for a wall-bouncing enemy, written by Kill_Wall_Bouncing_Enemy. Each frame, XOR-draws the entity using the stored animation colour, then increments the death animation counter. When it reaches 15 writes Process_Entity_Record_Next7 as the retire handler and jumps to it. Otherwise loads the counter into B', reads the entity Y position, selects a scroll offset (248 within the play area, 240 near the ceiling), updates the Y position, XOR-erases the entity, and jumps to Process_Entity_Record_Next6.
